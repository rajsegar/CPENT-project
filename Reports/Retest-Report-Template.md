## Status definitions

- **Fixed:** The original condition can no longer be reproduced, and the
  intended control works.
- **Partially fixed:** Risk is reduced, but some aspect remains exploitable or
  unverified.
- **Open:** The original condition is still reproducible.
- **Not tested:** The retest could not reach a reliable conclusion.
