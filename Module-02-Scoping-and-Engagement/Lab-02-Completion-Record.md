## Key Learning

I learned that a professional penetration test does not start with exploitation. It starts with permission, scope, Rules of Engagement, safety limits, testing windows, evidence handling, cleanup, and reporting expectations.

## Scope vs Rules of Engagement

```text
Scope = what and where I am allowed to test.
Rules of Engagement = how I am allowed to test it.
```
