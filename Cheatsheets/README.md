# Cheatsheets

Cheat sheets are fast review aids, not substitutes for the full notes or an
authorized lab.

## Available

- [Module 01: Introduction and Methodology](Module-01-Cheat-Sheet.md)

## Adding a cheat sheet

Keep it short and include:

- Essential terms.
- A small process or decision flow.
- Evidence and reporting reminders.
- Safety or scope limits.
- Links back to the full module.

Do not add live secrets, real targets, or unexplained destructive commands.
