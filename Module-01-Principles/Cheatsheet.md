# CPENT Module 01 — Cheat Sheet

## Penetration testing

Authorized simulated attack to test security controls and prove real risk.

## Vulnerability scanning

Automated process that finds known vulnerabilities.

## Main difference

Scanning finds issues.  
Penetration testing validates and chains issues to show real impact.

## Must remember

Authorization + scope + timing + rules of engagement.

## Three phases

Pre-Attack:
- Scope
- Recon
- Scanning
- Enumeration

Attack:
- Exploitation
- Privilege escalation
- Lateral movement
- Controlled access

Post-Attack:
- Cleanup
- Evidence
- Report
- Remediation advice

## Exam tip

CPENT wants practical thinking, not just memorization.

Always ask:

- What do I know?
- What do I need to know?
- What can I safely test?
- What is the impact?
- How do I report it?
